# Specification: SME Hub & Growth Roadmaps

## Purpose

A dedicated space providing SMEs with roadmaps, tech toolkits, and success playbooks to drive engagement and lead generation.

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Growth Roadmap Visualizer

The SME Hub SHALL feature a section outlining the stages of digital transformation for a PH SME.

- It SHALL include an interactive visual of "Startup -> Digitized -> Optimized -> Scale".

#### Scenario: Exploring growth

- **Given** I am in the SME Hub
- **When** I view the Growth Roadmap
- **Then** I see clear steps I need to take for each stage of my business growth.

### Requirement: Success Vault (Playbooks)

The SME Hub SHALL provide downloadable resources for specific industries.

- Playbooks SHALL be gated via email signup.

#### Scenario: Downloading a playbook

- **Given** I want the "AI for Customer Service" playbook
- **When** I enter my email in the signup form
- **Then** I receive access to the templated PDF/Playbook.

### Requirement: Tech Access Toolkit

The SME Hub SHALL include a curated list of enterprise tools made accessible.

- It SHALL display logos and descriptions of tools like Supabase, Base, and Replicable LGU systems.

#### Scenario: Viewing toolkits

- **Given** I am in the SME Hub
- **When** I scroll to the Tech Toolkit
- **Then** I see enterprise tools presented as accessible for SMEs.
